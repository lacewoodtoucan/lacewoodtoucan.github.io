Clock Care Tips

To keep your Lacewood Toucan wall clock looking beautiful for years to come:

  • Dust regularly with a soft, lint-free cloth
  • Avoid placing in direct sunlight to prevent color fading
  • Keep away from high humidity areas
  • Handle with clean hands to prevent oils from transferring to the velvet
  • Do not use chemical cleaners on the wooden or velvet surfaces
